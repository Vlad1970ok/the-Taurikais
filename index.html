FAQ

What are The Taurikai?

Taurikais are a collection of 10,000 algorithmically generated taurikais that live on the Solana blockchain.

How can I buy a Taurikai?

Taurikai happens on this site. When the Taurikais go live, connect your Solana wallet and then click Mint. Congrats, you are now a Taurikai. Specific steps are as follows:

  • Download a Solana Wallet (Phantom, Solflare, or Sollet).
  • Deposit SOL to your wallet. SOL can be purchased on an exchange such as Coinbase or FTX. You’ll then transfer your SOL from your exchange account to your Solana Wallet.
  • At the time of launch, connect your wallet to the Taurikais website. Once the sale is live, you’ll be able to click the Mint button.
  • Once you click Mint, approve the transaction and then you have joined the community. Your Taurikai will appear in your Solana wallet.

    • When is the launch? How much will it cost?

    We are launching in Q2 2022. Stay tuned for the exact date. The price will be 1 SOL per Taurikai.

    Is there a buy limit?

    You can purchase one Taurikai per transaction, but you can make as many transactions as you would like, while supplies last.
